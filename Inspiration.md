Biggest inspiration is Dragon Eclipse, a game where you have 3 creatures you choose at the start of a run, each with unique abilities that you combo together along with different cards to make a successful build and complete a run. Pokemon is the inspiration for capturing the creatures. The idea for managing your creatures positions is a mix of dragon eclipse (you can move your 3 creatures between the front middle and back positions) and inventory management games. The action speed mechanic is inspire by turn based games which, rather than having every character move at once, move based on an overall timer which fills up their action bar until they get their turn.
