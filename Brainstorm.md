A roguelike card based monster catcher game where you try to collect the creatures you fight against to add to your team. Creatures have different stats and abilities to give them different roles in your team. All creatures are out on the field at once, and all act at the end of your turn. Creature's action rate is based on their speed, with 100 units of time passing each time you end your turn. Along with catching the creatures you fight you can collect cards to use on your turns to help you team in different ways. Creatures are arranged in a grid, different creatures being different shapes, allowing them to be positioned strategically realtive to eachother and the enemy's attacks.
